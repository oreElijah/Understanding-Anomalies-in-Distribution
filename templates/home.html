Understanding Anomaly in Distributions

This mini-site demonstrates seven common probability distributions and how to spot anomalies (outliers). We generate 5 datasets for each distribution (≥500 points each). Outliers are highlighted in red using the IQR rule; normal points are blue.

Normal

Bell-shaped, symmetric distribution defined by mean and standard deviation. Many natural phenomena approximate normality.

Uniform

All values in an interval are equally likely. Flat probability across the range.

Exponential

Models time between independent events at a constant average rate. Right-skewed, memoryless.

Poisson

Binomial

Gamma
