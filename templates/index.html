{{name}} Distribution

{%if name=="Gamma"%}

Continuous positive distribution often used for waiting times and reliability (shape k, scale θ).

{%elif name=="Exponetial"%}

Models time between independent events at a constant average rate. Right-skewed, memoryless.

{%elif name=="Binomial"%}

Number of successes in n independent trials with success probability p. Discrete, bounded.

{%elif name=="Lognormal"%}

If log(X) is normal, X is lognormal. Positive-only and right-skewed (multiplicative processes).

{%elif name=="Normal"%}

Bell-shaped, symmetric distribution defined by mean and standard deviation. Many natural phenomena approximate normality.

{%elif name=="Poisson"%}

Discrete distribution for counts of events in a fixed interval, given an average rate (λ).

{%elif name=="Uniform"%}

All values in an interval are equally likely. Flat probability across the range.
